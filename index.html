Got questions?

Frequently asked questions

Is this QR code generator really free?

Yes, 100% free. No signup, no credit card, no hidden fees. Generate as many QR codes as you want.

Do the QR codes have a watermark?

No watermark at all. The QR codes you download are clean and ready to use anywhere — print, digital, or anywhere else.

Do QR codes expire?

The QR codes generated here are static codes — they never expire and work forever as long as the content they point to (like a URL) still exists.

What file formats can I download?

You can download your QR code as PNG (best for most uses) or SVG (best for printing at large sizes since it scales without losing quality).

How do I use a QR code for WiFi?

Choose the WiFi tab, enter your network name and password, generate the code, and print it or display it. Anyone who scans it with their phone camera will be connected to your WiFi instantly — no typing required.

Is my data safe?

Everything runs directly in your browser. Your URLs, passwords, and text are never sent to any server. We cannot see what you generate.
